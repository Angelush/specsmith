# Anthropic
Anthropic is a prominent artificial intelligence research company. They are known for their work in developing large language models, notably the Claude series. Their research frequently emphasizes AI safety and alignment, making them a recurring entity in discussions about the responsible development and deployment of advanced AI systems within this wiki.
