# Opus 4.7 and OpenAI 5.5 Made Your Prompting Style Obsolete.

**Video:** [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ogTLWGBc3cE](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ogTLWGBc3cE) • **ID:** `ogTLWGBc3cE` • **Playlists:** P1, P2

## Concepts covered

- [[concepts/advanced-prompting-techniques]] — The "AI question method" for ~100×-more-powerful agents: (1) convey intent like a flashlight beam (center + edges), (2) ask open-ended questions that make the AI synthesize "what good looks like" beyond an eval, (3) frame questions that force engagement with ALL the data plus your named thesis.
- [[concepts/prompting-and-skill-design]] — Prompt engineering is now table stakes (you get no credit); the shift is from a "prompt world" to a "question world," because just-ask-for-what-you-want only works if you already know what you want.
- [[concepts/ai-builder-mindset]] — Treat AI as a senior partner, not a junior one (the year's biggest mental-model shift); the communication patterns that get the most out of agents are the same ones that make you a good manager of people.
