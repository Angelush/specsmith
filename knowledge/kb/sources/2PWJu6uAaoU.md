# The Real Problem With AI Agents Nobody's Talking About

**Video:** [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2PWJu6uAaoU](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2PWJu6uAaoU) • **ID:** `2PWJu6uAaoU` • **Playlists:** P0, P2

## Concepts covered

- [[concepts/agent-philosophy-and-mindset]] — Agents require detailed identity, context, and rules to be productive, as they don't function effectively right after a quick setup.
- [[concepts/multi-agent-system-design]] — Multi-agent systems thrive when each agent maintains a distinct identity, tools, and jurisdiction, as shared context can lead to dysfunction.
- [[concepts/knowledge-work-delegation]] — Before deploying a productive agent, first use a specialized interview agent to extract and define tacit knowledge.
- [[concepts/ai-quality-control]] — Senior expertise, though most beneficial for agent delegation, is challenging to capture because expert knowledge is often tacit and difficult to articulate, highlighting a structural trap in AI deployment.
